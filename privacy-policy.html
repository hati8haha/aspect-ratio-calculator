Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Aspect Ratio Calculator ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

We collect information about your interactions with our web application, including:

  • Information about your browser and device
  • Your IP address
  • Pages you visit and features you use within our application
  • The duration of your visit
  • Your clicks, movements, scrolls, and keystroke activity

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our web application and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Diagnose technical problems
  • Understand how users interact with our features

Visitor Recording

We use Hotjar, a behavioral analytics service, to record anonymized user sessions to understand how users interact with our application. This recording may include your mouse movements, clicks, scrolls, and keystrokes (excluding in password and form fields that may contain sensitive data).

This information helps us identify usability issues and improve our interface. The recordings do not identify you personally and sensitive information is automatically masked.

Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Store your privacy preferences
  • Enable visitor recording and analytics functionality

Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies.

You can opt out of Hotjar tracking by enabling the "Do Not Track" setting in your browser or by visiting: https://www.hotjar.com/legal/compliance/opt-out

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us
